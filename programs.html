Programs

These are the main steps and programs we plan to run in Bangladesh.

How a rescue works

  1. Someone reports an animal (location + condition)
  2. We assess urgency and plan a response
  3. Vet care and recovery support
  4. Sterilise/vaccinate when safe
  5. Foster care or adoption support

Program list

  • Rescue Response: emergency pickup and safe transport
  • Medical Care: treatment, medicine, and rehabilitation
  • Sterilisation: reduce street births and suffering
  • Community Feeding: basic food/water points
  • Foster & Adoption: safe homes with simple screening
