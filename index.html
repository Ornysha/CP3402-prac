StreetPaws Rescue Bangladesh

We help stray cats and dogs in Bangladesh who are injured, starving, or mistreated. Our goal is to rescue, provide medical support, and help them live a safer life.

What we do

  • Rescue animals in danger (based on reports)
  • Support vet treatment and recovery
  • Promote sterilisation and vaccination
  • Foster and adoption support
